About Campus Event Tracker

Your one-stop platform for discovering and managing campus events

Our Mission

Campus Event Tracker aims to centralize event management for college students, faculty, and organizers. We make it easy to discover workshops, sports events, cultural programs, and club activities happening on campus.

Who We Serve

  • College Students
  • Faculty Members
  • Event Organizers
  • Club Leaders

Key Features

Event Discovery

Browse and search for upcoming campus events by type, date, and location.

Easy RSVP

One-click RSVP system with attendance tracking and duplicate prevention.

Smart Reminders

Automatic email reminders sent 24 hours before your RSVP'd events.

Event Creation

Create and manage your own events with detailed information and attendee tracking.

Advanced Search

Filter events by type (Sports, Cultural, Workshop, Academic) and search by title.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design that works perfectly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
